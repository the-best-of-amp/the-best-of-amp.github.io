Prayer of the Minimum Wage Burger

Anonymous || &amp 001

Lord, I Beseech Three:

Let my commute be painless

and my toil be for wholesome ends.

If I should stray,

to you I pray

to keep me on task.

And all I ask

is that I have my family tend,

(Alas, alas!)

to my inevitable workplace injury.
